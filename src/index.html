Vildskud / Sustainable digital solution
Read More

Vildskud is a independent theater festival in Copenhagen.

During this group project we were tasked with creating an user friendly website, optimized for for mobile view.

Vildskud requested for the site to be sustainable, with each page being under 0.125 mb, as well as for us to make it easier for users to get an overview of the ticket information, the festival program and where to find the different venues.

SILFEN / digital solution, Video & some guide
Read More

Silfen is a Danish accessory and clothing brand that mainly focuses on designing handbags, one of the brand’s core values is sustainability.

SILFEN approached us looking for a way to communicate their sustainability values and initiatives in an accessible and honest way across all platforms.

To do this we developed a new sustainability page to be included on Silfens existing website. We also created a promotional video that put focus on Silfens sustainability actions as well as a social media strategy with suggestions on how to communicate their sustainability initiatives through instagram.

Customise Me / Digital solution & style guide
Read More

CustomiseMe is a company that creates customized hand soaps made out of sustainable and vegan materials.

CustomiseMe asked for a new more user friendly website that would represent them as a sustainable brand, and put focus on their soap customisation process.

My group created a new visual identity based on user research and benchmarking. We also shot new product photos to create visual coherence throughout the website.

Furthermore we created high fidelity prototype with a complete redesign of their customise soap ordering process.
