Tag Snakken | Digital Concept

Concept developememnt - wireframing - ux/ui design - Figma

Link to Figma Prototype: Tag Snakken app prototype

Description: A digital concept developed for the insurance company Gjensidige, aimed at strengthening their presence in todays evolving trends and challenges within the healthcare sector. Focusing on youth and substance abuse prevention, our core idea is to create a digital tool to help parents understand and prevent their children's use of addictive substances. This concept takes the form of a mobile app prototype for parents with children aged 10-15. It would leverage data collection, artificial intelligence, customized content, and tailored features to facilitate open and secure conversations between parents and their children.
