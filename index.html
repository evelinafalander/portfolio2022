I’m Evelina, a digital design student from Sweden, currently in my 3rd semester at Copenhagen school of design and technology (kea https://kea.dk/en/). During my studies I have learned to design, develop, plan and produce multimedia products including websites, videos and social media content. I have worked with client based cases and produced websites and content according to a brief. With my education I hope to in the future work with digital design at an agency with like minded people and develop customized digital solutions and content for clients.
